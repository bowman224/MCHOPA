Classic Series
Gregory Mchopa's classic series is comprised of portraiture paintings that capture the iconic Masai aesthetic; long elegant body lines, flowing robes (called Shúkà in Maa or Kanga in Swahili) in their favored red and yellow earth tones set in a background of the expansive and colorful East African plains.

Illustrative Series
The illustrative series provides a glimpse into the rhythms of life in the nomadic warrior culture. Depictions of the circular Inkajijik (houses) built by able-bodied women, snow-capped Mt. Kilimanjaro and shade providing acacia trees pepper the paintings of daily life.

Browse the Illustrative Series
Abstract Series
With his abstract series, Mchopa evokes the strong emotions of cultural pluralism that marks the Masai identity. Forceful brush strokes lend an air of organic movement to the sweeping colors and contrasts, as life moves past reality and into the dreamscape.
